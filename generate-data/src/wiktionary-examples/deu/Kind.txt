<div class="mw-heading mw-heading4"><h4 id="Usage_notes">Usage notes</h4><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kind&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Usage notes"><span>edit</span></a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></div>
<ul><li>The normal plural is <i><b>Kinder</b></i>. The double plural <i class="Latn mention" lang="de"><a href="/wiki/Kinders#German" title="Kinders">Kinders</a></i> (also <i class="Latn mention" lang="de"><a href="/wiki/Kinners#German" title="Kinners">Kinners</a></i>) is colloquial and chiefly restricted to Low German areas (northern Germany). It is most often heard as a <a href="/wiki/vocative" title="vocative">vocative</a>, either referring to an actual group of children or figuratively: <i>Kinders, wie die Zeit vergeht!</i> − “Boy, how time flies!”</li>
<li>Dialectal diminutives include <i class="Latn mention" lang="de"><a href="/wiki/Kindel#German" title="Kindel">Kindel</a></i>, <i class="Latn mention" lang="de"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kindele&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Kindele (page does not exist)">Kindele</a></i>, <i class="Latn mention" lang="de"><a href="/wiki/Kindl#German" title="Kindl">Kindl</a></i>, <i class="Latn mention" lang="de"><a href="/wiki/Kindle#German" title="Kindle">Kindle</a></i> and <i class="Latn mention" lang="de"><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kindli&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Kindli (page does not exist)">Kindli</a></i>.</li>
<li>In German law <i>Kind</i> is usually defined as a person under 14 years of age,<sup id="cite&#95;ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>1<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> while in non-German law <i>Kind</i> can mean <i>a person under 18 years of age</i>.<sup id="cite&#95;ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span class="cite-bracket">&#91;</span>2<span class="cite-bracket">&#93;</span></a></sup> See also <i class="Latn mention" lang="de"><a href="/wiki/Jugendlicher#German" title="Jugendlicher">Jugendlicher</a></i> (person under 18 years but at least 14 years old) and <i class="Latn mention" lang="de"><a href="/wiki/Minderj%C3%A4hriger#German" title="Minderjähriger">Minderjähriger</a></i> (person under 18 years of age).</li></ul>
